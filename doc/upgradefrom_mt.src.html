{{{Upgrading from Movable Type}}}

{{{Before you begin, please note that some network features of b2evolution require you to agree our simple Terms of service.}}}

{{{This procedure has been tested with Movable Type version 2.661 but should also work with other versions. You can't break anything by trying anyway...}}}

  1. {{{Use MT's export functionnality to create a .TXT file containing your posts.}}}
  2. {{{Install b2evolution in the regular way.}}}
  3. {{{Once b2evo is up and running, copy your .TXT export file into the /blogs/admin folder on your server.}}}
  4. {{{In the admin, go to the Tools tab and launch the MT migration utility.}}}
  5. {{{Follow the instructions on screen.}}}
  6. {{{Import will only take a few seconds/minutes depending on how many posts you import.}}}
