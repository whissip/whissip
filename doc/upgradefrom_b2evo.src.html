{{{Upgrading from a previous version of b2evolution}}}

{{{This page will be updated. Don't waste time translating this...}}}

  1. {{{Backup all b2evolution files, including /conf, skins, blog templates and stubfiles for your blogs. You should also backup your database if you can.}}}
  2. {{{Replace all b2evolution files, including /conf, skins, blog templates and stubfiles with the new ones; (This is important in order to activate all security fixes!) If you have customized your blog template/skin, please use the provided demo templates/skins until the end of the upgrade process. You'll find instructions on how to upgrade your customized templates/skins at the end of this procedure.}}}
  3. {{{Redo your configuration in the /conf folder as you did previously.}}} {{{As always, the only requirement is to edit _config.php. The other config files can be used without modification (provided you use the new ones!), however you should have look inside for fine tuning once your new version is running.}}}
  4. {{{Upload the b2evolution files to your website, according to the URL you have just configured.}}}
  5. {{{Upload the new files to your server, and then run the database tables upgrade on your server!}}}
  6. {{{On production servers, it is recommended that you delete the /blogs/install/ subfolder.}}}
  7. {{{Just in case you did not do it before, you may want to check out the tricky stuff. (online manual)}}}
  8. {{{(If you had custom skins or templates, follow the online instructions about upgrading them from version 0.8.0.x or from version 0.8.2.x. (online manual)}}}
