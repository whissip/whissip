Upgrading from b2evolution version 0.8.0

As always, it is very wise to do a backup of your current website / blog / database (depending on what you already have) before starting to install something new.

Upgrading from version 0.8.0:

  1. Backup all b2evolution files, including /conf, skins, blog templates and stubfiles for your blogs. You should also backup your database if you can.
  2. Replace all b2evolution files with the new ones, including /conf, skins, blog templates and stubfiles for your blogs;
  3. Redo your configuration as you did previously. As always, the only requirement is to edit b2evo_config.php. The other config files can be used without modification, however you should have look inside for fine tuning once your new version is running;
  4. Upload the files to your website, according to the URL you have just configured.
  5. Upload the new files to your server, and then run the database tables upgrade on your server! Once your database is upgraded, it will still work with previous version of b2evolution if for some reason you decide to switch back.
  6. Test
  7. Just in case you did not do it before, you may want to check out the tricky stuff. (online manual)
  8. If you had customized your blog template/skins, please refer to the online manual about upgrading templates/skins from 0.8.0. (online manual)
