Upgrading from b2evolution version 0.8.6 or later

As always, it is very wise to do a backup of your current website / blog / database (depending on what you already have) before starting to install something new.

Upgrading from version 0.8.6 is straightforward:

  1. You can keep your /conf, skins, blog templates and stubfiles for your blogs. However, now may be a good time to back them up ;)
  2. Just replace the b2evolution core files. That means the following folders: /admin, /b2evocore, /htsrv, /locales, /xmlsrv.
  3. Although it is not mandatory, you can also update the _antispam.php, _formatting.php and _locales.php files in the /conf folder.
  4. Thats's it.
