Upgrading from b2evolution version 0.8.2-RC2 or 0.8.2-RC3

As always, it is very wise to do a backup of your current website / blog / database (depending on what you already have) before starting to install something new.

Upgrading from version 0.8.2-RC2 or RC3 is very easy:

  • You can keep your database as it is (no upgrade is necessary);
  • You can keep your /conf as it is;
  • You can keep your skins & templates as they are;
  • Just replace the application files (all the other files: admin, b2evocore, htsrv, xmlsrv, etc...) with the new ones and you're done ;)
